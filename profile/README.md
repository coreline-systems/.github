# Coreline Systems Limited

Coreline Systems Limited is a Hong Kong–based research and technology company focused on the systems, strategies, and infrastructure that support long-term capital development.

Our work explores how limited capital can be allocated, compounded, and scaled through disciplined research, quantitative methods, automation, and risk management. We also study how established capital can maintain resilience, efficiency, and durable strategic advantages across changing market conditions.
